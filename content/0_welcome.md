# Welcome

This is an **educational site** that explains how **RAG** (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) works from a technical perspective, with a focus on **Graph RAG**: its architectures, components, and how they fit together.

## What you will find here

- **Architectures** — Different ways to design RAG and Graph RAG systems (pipelines, agents, hybrid setups).
- **Key Concepts** — Building blocks: chunking, embeddings, vector stores, graph databases, LLMs, and how they connect.
